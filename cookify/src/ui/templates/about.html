About Cookify

Cookify is an advanced AI-powered system that extracts structured recipes from cooking videos.

Have you ever watched a cooking video and wished you had a written recipe to follow? Cookify solves this problem by automatically analyzing cooking videos and extracting detailed, structured recipes including ingredients, quantities, cooking steps, and more.

Our system uses state-of-the-art computer vision and natural language processing techniques to understand what's happening in cooking videos, identify ingredients and actions, and organize everything into a clear, easy-to-follow recipe format.

How It Works

Cookify processes cooking videos through a sophisticated pipeline of AI models:

1. Video Preprocessing

Videos are split into frames and audio for parallel processing. Frames are sampled at optimal rates to balance accuracy and performance.

2. Frame Analysis

Computer vision models detect objects, recognize text, identify cooking actions, and segment the video into meaningful scenes.

3. Audio Analysis

Speech recognition transcribes narration, while NLP models extract cooking instructions, ingredients, and measurements from the text.

4. Multimodal Integration

Visual and audio information is combined to create a comprehensive understanding of the cooking process.

5. Recipe Extraction

Structured recipe data is extracted, including title, servings, ingredients with quantities, and step-by-step instructions.

6. Output Formatting

The extracted recipe is formatted into a clean, user-friendly presentation with timestamps linked to the original video.
